Big Game Hunting

Montana's big game hunting is second to none. We offer highly personable and quality hunts with a maximum of four hunters on guided hunts and six on drop camps, restricting it to one camp per area at a time. For the guided bow hunts and mountain lion hunts, we hold to a maximum of two hunters at a time to ensure quality. The pack-in hunts require strenuous physical exertion while the ranch hunts require much less effort and are more accessible.

Our big game hunts include Elk, Mule Deer, White-tailed Deer, Bear, Moose, Mountain Lion, Sheep, and Wolf.

Float Trips / Fishing

Our float trips are based out of our ranch in Superior, Montana. Multi-day trips spend each night there in a modern house with home-cooked meals. We float the Clark Fork River with over 70 miles of scenic beauty and adventure.

Whitewater: Our whitewater trips run the famed Alberton Gorge, which boasts over 20 rapids and offers a new adventure each trip.
Leisure Floats: Relax and enjoy the river, swim, view the scenary and wildlife. We are licensed for the Clark Fork, Bitterroot, and Blackfoot Rivers, as well as most of the other rivers in Western Montana.
Float Fishing: The Rainbow, Cutthroat, and Brown Trout fight to get on your hook on this stretch of the river. We are licensed for the Clark Fork, Bitteroot, and Blackfoot Rivers.

Summer Pack Trips

During the summer months, we offer wilderness excursions designed entirely around your preferences. Some of the options that you might wish to integrate into your family vacation include scenic and wildlife photography, lake and stream fishing, hiking, trail riding, and camping, among others. These excursions are appropriate for families, couples, or groups.

We arrange these entirely from your plans and desires when you tell us what you would like from your wilderness excursion. Our most popular trip over the last couple of years has been:

  • 3 days/2 nights in the mountains (horseback riding, camping, mountain lake fishing)
  • 1 day white water float through the famed Alberton Gorge
  • 1 day leisure or fishing float on the Clark Fork River
The main emphasis on all of our trips is sharing in our joy of providing a true western experience.

Trail Rides

We do trail rides both out of our Superior base and our Fish Creek base. Miles and miles of trail, with a different experience on each of them. Let us know what you are looking for, and we will set up a specific ride for your group.

  • Full Day: 5 - 8 hours depending on where we go (lunch included)
  • 1/2 Day: 2 - 4 hours depending on desires of riders
