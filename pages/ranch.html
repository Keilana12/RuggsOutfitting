River Ranch

Horse Leasing

Our horses are suited for trail rides, back country and pleasure riding. We use these horses with all aspects of our own outfitting business. Having sold some of our outfitting areas but still wanting to work with the horse side we started leasing horses in 2010. They have been in kids camps in Idaho, with several different outfitters in both Montana and Idaho and with do-it-yourself clients. We match the horses for the type riding you will be doing and the experience level of the riders.

Cabin Rental

We have two cabins right alongside the Clark Fork river. These cabins were built in 2005 and are near our Horse Motel. The cabins can be combined with other activities for a package deal.

We have now added (for the more traditional adventurous) a TeePee. This is an 18' TeePee which lets you experience the old way without being too far from modern facilities. Great view of the Clark Fork River.

Horse Boarding

We are able to board horses by the day or month. Our corrals have automatic heated water with feeders and shelters. They are 50' x 150' giving plenty of room to run.

  • Trailer parking:
    • Free: no electricity with corral rental
    • $10/night: electricity with corral rental
    • $15/night: electricity without corral rental
  • Vet and Farrier available on-call
  • Horse training and conditioning available
  • Plenty of riding area, both private and USFS
  • Riverside Cabins nearby available for rent
