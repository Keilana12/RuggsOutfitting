We offer horses to lease to outfitters, kids camps and individuals. Our horses are suited for trail rides, back country and pleasure riding. We use these horses with all aspects of our own outfitting business. They have been in kid and church camps in Washington, Idaho, and Montana, with several different outfitters and do-it-yourself clients.
